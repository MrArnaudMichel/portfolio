## About MoseArt

MoseArt is a management simulation inspired by the real-life role of an IUT director. Each turn represents a quarter in a 5-year mandate. You balance budgets, satisfaction, and academic success, facing random events like pandemics, grants, or scandals.

## Gameplay & Features

- Manage finances, satisfaction, and success rates
- Invest in renovations, launch new programs, recruit staff
- Make choices during events (e.g. pandemic, cold wave, grants)
- Interactive campus map and performance gauges
- End-of-turn summaries and evolving scenarios

### Scenarios

- **Sandbox:** Develop your IUT freely
- **Academic Excellence:** Reach 100% success in 5 years
- **Satisfaction:** Maximize well-being for all
- **Sustainable Development:** Renovate all buildings without going bankrupt

## Educational Goals

- Learn resource management and strategic planning
- Discover the director’s responsibilities and long-term impact of decisions
- Understand public relations and academic development
